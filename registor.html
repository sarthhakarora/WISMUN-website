INSTRUCTIONS FOR EXTERNAL (GSG/NON-WITTY) DELEGATES

1. Click the Link

Click on the GSG/Non-Witty Registrations link at the top of this page. You will be redirected to a Google Form for external delegates.

2. Provide Details

Fill in the required details correctly in the form. (Google sign-in may be required)

3. Delegate Code & Payment Info

After submitting the form, you’ll receive an email with your delegate code and the bank details for payment.

4. Make Payment

Complete your payment using the bank details shared with you via email.

5. Submit Payment Confirmation Form

Once payment is done, fill out the Payment Confirmation Form (link provided in your email). You'll need to upload your payment screenshot and enter your delegate code.

6. Confirmation Email

Once your payment is verified, you'll receive an email confirming your successful registration and selection for WISMUN 2025.

7. Committee Allocation

Shortly after, you will receive another email with your committee allocation to begin your MUN journey.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR WITTY DELEGATES

1. Click the Link

Click on the Witty Registrations link at the top of this page to open the registration form.

2. Provide Details

Fill in your correct details in the Google Form. (Google sign-in may be required)

3. Get Your Delegate Code

After submitting the form, you'll receive a unique delegate code via email. Keep this code safe for the payment step.

4. Shortlisting Email

Your application will go through a shortlisting process. If selected, you'll receive an email confirming your selection.

5. Visit Front Office

Visit the school’s front office with your delegate code and complete the registration payment.

6. Submit Payment Confirmation Form

After payment, fill out the Payment Confirmation Form (link provided in your email) with your delegate code and payment screenshot.

7. Registration Success

Once your payment is verified, you will receive an email confirming your successful registration for WISMUN 2025.
